How We Met

Rachel and I first met at a dinner party hosted by Rachel and her friends. I brought over burnt chocolate chip cookies and later asked for her number in order to "borrow a textbook" for one of our Systems Engineering classes. Shortly thereafter, Rachel and I had our first date together, during which I lost my phone. She graciously helped me search for it across Charlottesville (sadly, we never found it). We officially started dating a few months after and the rest is history.

Events

Saturday, October 26th

Wedding Ceremony 5PM - 6PM

The wedding ceremony will be held outdoors at Mount Ida Farms. The ceremony will be officiated by Rachel and Sam's close friend TJ Phillips. Buses will depart from The Draftsman hotel at 4pm.

Cocktail Hour and Reception 6PM - 11PM

The celebration begins. Music, drinking, and dancing all night celebrating Rachel and Sam. Buses will depart from Mount Ida, returning to downtown Charlottesville at The Draftsman at 11pm. Rachel and Sam invite guests to continue to celebrate in downtown Charlottesville.

Friday, October, 25th

Friday Night Reception 7PM-9PM

Rachel and Sam will be holding a reception on Friday night at the Tasting Room at Mount Ida Farms for any and all wedding guests to attend. Drinks and hors d'oeuvres will be provided. Shuttle bus transportation will be provided back to The Draftsman and Hampton Inn hotels.

Sunday, October, 27th

Farewell Brunch 9AM-11AM

A farewell brunch will be held at The Draftsman event room the morning after the ceremony and reception. A buffet-style breakfast and drinks will be provided.
